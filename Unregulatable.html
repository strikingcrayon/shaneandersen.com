I worry long term, so you can focus short term.
I keep money and madness out of the way of your meaning.

I operate a proprietorship which secures time as net profit. I officiate my family's Economic Ark.
I am a freelance brain for hire. A redacted advisor. A manager, and a solarpunk fixer.
Imagine a family docotor but for wealth instead of health.
An accountant and a lawyer, mixed into one, who is most assuredly neither.
A therapist, who cares more about your best self, than whoever you are today.

Who do I help?

Generally: Those who can manage frank introspection, honest communication, and a good faith subjugation of their Ego.
Specifically: Those who can answer in the affirmative to at least one of the three following questions:

Do you command your own time?
AKA: Do you have extra hours to spend?

Do you have an excess of capital?
AKA: Can you afford to pay me?

Can you speak Truth and shackle your demons?
AKA: Are you brave? Are you Good?

Customers must answer in the affirmative to one of the above questions.
Clients must answer in the affirmative to two of the above questions.
